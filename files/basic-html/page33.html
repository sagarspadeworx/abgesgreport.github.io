          Since the beginning of the pandemic, UTCL's wide-ranging COVID response have
          included the distribution of 237,197 masks, 428,798 soaps, 21,252 hand sanitisers for
          the general public and 1,058 PPE kits for frontline workers. As a curative measure, the
          company has supplied 629 oxygen cylinders, 5 oxygen concentrators and 5 oxygen
          plants; set up 8 emergency treatment centres and hosted 2,638 awareness camps;
          and mobilised 17,797 cooked food and 9,048 dry ration kits for the migrant workers
          and rural areas.

          An innovative way in which UTCL helped the community was through distribution of
          22,000 'potlis' across 15 unit location. Each 'potli' is a physical raw mix of Camphor
          and Carom seeds (ajwain) tied in a small piece of cloth. Smelling this mixture is
          believed to alleviate nasal congestion.
